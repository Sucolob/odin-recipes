Vegan Burger

Description

A vegan burger, where you use vegetal protein instead of animal protein, is an excellent alternative to the usual burger. We will be following my wife's recipe... sort of.

In this recipe we will be using lentils as "meat" for the burger. Lentils are very healthy grains with almost as much protein content as red meat while also being more healthy.

Also, our primary objective here is to make the patty, not the actual burger. That part is left to the reader's discretion. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 500 g of Lentils
  • 250 g of Leek
  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 branches of Coriander
  • 1 head of Garlic
  • 30 g of Wheat bran
  • Water as needed
  • Oil of preference
  • Salt/Pepper/Spices to taste
