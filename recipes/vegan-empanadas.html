Vegan Empanadas

Description

"Empanadas" is the name of a typical Latin-American dish where you have a dough exterior with a (usually) salty filling. These are most often deep fried and filled with assorted meats and, commonly, rice or potato.

The dough, or crust, is most commonly of wheat, but you can also find empanadas with cassava, corn, or even potato crust. Empanadas can also be baked, though they are not as common.
